## Long description

Track flights and airports in real time with RouteWings. See departures and arrivals for major hubs, open any flight for details, and follow aircraft on a live map when data is available (Premium).

Share flights with family using private tracking links — perfect for coordinating pickups and peace of mind. Enable push notifications for delays, gate changes, and important updates (where supported by your device and our data sources).

**Free:** airport boards, flight lookup, saved flights (limits apply), core alerts.  
**Premium:** more saved and push-tracked flights, family links, live map on supported flights, deeper board tools — billed via subscription; cancel anytime.

Flight information comes from third-party sources and may be delayed or incomplete. Always confirm with your airline.
